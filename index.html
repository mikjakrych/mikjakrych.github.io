Mission

As a conservative community of believers, we strive to follow Jesus in our everyday lives. Our mission is to disciple new believers, develop intermediate believers, and dispatch mature believers.

Doctrine

Arminian in background, our church generally embraces a less Calvinistic or Lutheran system of thinking, acknowledging, however, the contributions of Calvin, Luther, and other famous reformed teachers. As a complementarian congregation, we hold the belief that God has destined men to be the leaders of the church (1 Timothy 3). We also believe that God has revealed Himself through His divine Word, the infallible Bible. Our basic beliefs are summed up in the Apostles Creed. Though conservative in thought, we appreciate believers of all denominations and welcome you to our services.
