Before You Begin

  • Crystal Grower v2 is just a video game (don't spend too much time on it!). If you are intrested in REAL crystal growing, do some research on how crystals are made and how they are used.
  • Read the following instructions before you begin playing, then reload the game. Have fun!

Playing the Game

  • Press and hold the spacebar to make your crystal seed float up. Let go to make it fall.
  • Try to catch the solutions (green drops). The solutes in the drops will precipitate onto your crystal and make it grow larger, increasing the value of your crystal. The amount your crystal grows depends on the size of the droplet.
  • Try not to hit the solvents (blue drips). The solvents will dissolve your crystal, making it shrink and decreasing your crystal worth. Once again, the amount the solvent shrinks your crystal depends on the size of the solvent drop.
  • Try all you can to avoid the red boxes--they will shatter your crystal and your crystal's value will be absolutely ZERO.
  • When all the objects have passed the screen, the game is over. Check out the value of your crystal on the top left corner of the screen. If it's positive, you're crystal was worth making. If it's negative, you spent more money making the crystal than it's worth :( .
  • Reload this page to restart the game.
