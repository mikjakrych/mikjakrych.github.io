A truly perfect location for your lifestyle

The Briarwood Apartments offers 2 and 3 bedroom floor plans to suit your needs,
surrounded by relaxing suburban landscaping and located just minutes from
Western Washington University and Downtown Bellingham.

These apartments offer a safe and friendly environment for a comfortable
home, designed with a larger living area making these apartments a step above the rest.

"Spacious Living, Higher Quality of Life"

4101 Consolidation Ave, Bellingham, WA 98229


 
From I-5 North
Take Exit 252, turn left at the light off of the off-ramp and another
left onto Lincoln St.  Take a right onto Byron St. and a left onto
Ashley Ave.  Finally take a right onto Consolidation Ave and the
Briarwood Apartments will be on your right.

From I-5 South
Take Exit 252, turn left at the stop sign after the off-ramp onto
Lincoln St. Take a right onto Byron St. just before the Park-n-Ride,
and a left onto Ashley Ave.  Finally take a right onto Consolidation
Ave and the Briarwood Apartments will be on your right.
