Crystal Grower v3

Before you begin: Crystal Grower v3 is just a video game (don't spend too much time on it!). If you are intrested in real crystal growing, do some research on how crystals are made and how they are used. The instructions are as follows:

  • Click and hold the mouse button over the game area to make the crystal seed float up. (This is more difficult on tap screen devices.)
  • Try to catch the solution droplets (green). The solute material will precipitate onto your crystal and make it grow larger. The amount your crystal grows depends on the size of the droplet.
  • Avoid the water droplets (blue). These will dissolve some of your crystal away reducing the value. Once again, the amount the solvent shrinks your crystal depends on the size of the solvent drop.
  • Try all you can to avoid the blocks of dynamite. They will shatter your crystal, whose value becomes absolutely zero.
