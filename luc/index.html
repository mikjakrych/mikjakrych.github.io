What is Permaculture?
What is permaculture? This science can be called "sustainable agriculture." Someone once said, "There are three important values in the realms of permaculture: 'people care,' 'earth care,' and 'return of surplus.'" "People care" refers to the fact the permaculture must help people. "Earth care" represents the importance of nurturing Nature. "Return of surplus" is putting extra stuff (like food scraps) back into nature. WOW! What incredible values.
Slogan.
