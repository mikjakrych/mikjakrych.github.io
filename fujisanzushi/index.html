About us

Chef Billy Chen has over 13 years of culinary experience. He has been offering sushi in Bellingham Public Market since 2006. And since the Public Market closed, we moved to here.

We Provide the freshest sushi, delicious Japanese cuisine, Japanese craft beer, and sake selection.

We understand as a friendly neighborhood restaurant, bring the best tastes out of the freshness and cleanness is the most important success and most valuable trust to our customers.

We strive everyday to provide every customer with great anticipation and expectations for a wonderful meal leaving them satisfied and looking forward to their next visit.
